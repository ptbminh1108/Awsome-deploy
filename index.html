📚 How to Play Math Fun Adventure
🎯 Game Goal

Solve math problems correctly to earn points and become a Math Master!

🎮 How to Play
  1. Choose your difficulty level (Easy, Medium, or Hard)
  2. Read each math problem carefully
  3. Click on the correct answer from the 4 choices
  4. Get instant feedback - correct answers earn points!
  5. Complete all 10 questions to see your final score
⭐ Scoring
  • Easy: 10 points per correct answer
  • Medium: 20 points per correct answer
  • Hard: 30 points per correct answer
  • Bonus points for speed in timed mode!
⏰ Timed Challenge

Turn on the timer for an extra challenge! You'll have 60 seconds to answer as many questions as possible.
